AMC 10 - Adaptive Practice

Adaptive Practice works by using a model based on a chess ELO system that assigns you questions with similar difficulty to your level within a range. Problems that are of a much higher difficulty reward you with more points and take away less when incorrect, with the opposite true for easier problems. Your "ELO" is displayed in the top right corner. You can also advance your practice through spaced repetition, where incorrect problems will appear to reinforce your learning. The Topics Glossary is there for your convenience so that you can best understand what and how to study as well as practice topics you are often weak on.
