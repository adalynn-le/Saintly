Change Log

Stay up to date with all of Saintly's changes and updates! (Also sort of just a diary for me to track progress but yk opensource)

July 13th 2026
Pathways + ZeroSet + Donations!
Saintly is introducing Learning Pathways! Get a personalized and tailored learning experience that takes you, in order, through all of the topics covered on the AMC 10. We are debuting this with Algebra an dhope to establish more in the future! We are also partnering with ZeroSet, an innovative platform for proof-based math sharing. Saintly is now a nonprofit under Hack Club Fiscal Sponsorship. With your donations, we will be able to reach new levels and grow our platform to help more students!

June 27th 2026
Timers
In order to prepare for the high-speed pressure of the AMC 10, users can now track their average time per question in order to ensure that they are prepared for the time constraints of the AMC 10.

Saintly \(\times\) Solvefire + Topic Tracking

ELO system for Adaptive
Adaptive Practice was updated to transform typical, strict question difficulty based sourcing into an adaptive system mirroring the chess ELO system. Questions are generated randomly within range to your own score and adjust in diffculty by range and accuracy.
