Change Log

Stay up to date with all of Saintly's changes and updates! (Also sort of just a diary for me to track progress but yk opensource)
If you have any suggestions for updates, please contact us at saintly.math@gmail.com
circle_circle
May 15th 2026
Saintly \(\times\) STEMise \(\times\) YIMO
Saintly is proud to announce our partnership with STEMise, a nonprofit built around connecting students to free and accescible STEM education. We are also proud sponsors of their flagship competition Youth International Math Olympiad. You can now train for YIMO on Saintly with our adaptive learning and detailed analytics.
circle
May 10th 2026
Improved Local Storage
Your stats will now be saved from your last session on Saintly's adaptive practice!
circle
May 9th 2026
Saintly \(\times\) Solvefire + Topic Tracking
Saintly is proud to announce a partnership with Solvefire!. You can now see past solvefire questions with Saintly's adaptive topic tracking and in depth metrics. Saintly now also allows you to track your performance with new visual data including heatmaps tracking your accuracy compared to others, and charts that track your most mistaken topics, mistakes, and subjects.
circle
May 2nd 2026
Topics Dash
Developed two fully fledged articles on Word Problems and Algebraic Manipulation so that you can further your learning and growth. Accescible through Topics Dashboard.
circle
April 28th 2026
Leaderboard
You can now see a database of the hardest questions by accuracy according to user data, and try them on your own. Check it out on the adaptive practice page!
circle
April 26th 2026
Daily Problems Streaks
Want to monitor your progress? The Daily Problem now has streaks to keep you coming every day
circle
April 24th 2026
Hints and Steps + Daily Problem
In response to past advice, Saintly has added a strike based reveal system that allows you to have multiple tries and see hints/the first few steps before giving up. We also added a Daily Problem system so you can get a new challenge every day
circle
April 15th 2026
Volunteer Application
Saintly cannot function alone! If you want to help out, check out the heart and hand icon in the top right to look at volunteering opportunities
circle
April 14th 2026
ELO system for Adaptive
Adaptive Practice was updated to transform typical, strict question difficulty based sourcing into an adaptive system mirroring the chess ELO system. Questions are generated randomly within range to your own score and adjust in diffculty by range and accuracy.
circle
April 12th 2026
Spaced Repetition + Forms
Created a contact form and a submit a solution form (powered by formspree!) to enable easy communication and allow you to have a part in Saintly! Also added an algorithim for spaced repetition in adaptive practice order to ensure that you are getting enough practices on questions you get wrong. Fixed a few minor mobile issues with the error 404 page and adaptive buttons, and rearranged styling and text.
