Our Partners

Saintly cannot function on its own. We are lucky to be supported by organizations and educational institutions that allow us to collaborate with them to build a better system for all students. These collaborations also allow us to source novel problems and questions in order to continuously challenge you and prepare you for other competitions. Please feel free to check out any of our collaborators websites or check their dedicated Saintly Practice Pages where you can preview some of their coursework and questions in partnership with Saintly's analytics and adaptive study.

Solvefire is the optimal website for interactive, worldwide math competitions weekly. They host contests meant to challenge in a collaborative setting and prepare you for international olympiads. Past exams are also published for student use and featured on Saintly to ensure access to adaptive practice.

STEMise is an international nonprofit built to create equity in STEM and promote a love of the subjects involved without a high cost. They create and transport STEM kits worldwide and provide workshops and classes for people across the world. Their latest project, Youth International Math Olympiad, seeks to incorporate people from around the world into competitive math without the cost.
