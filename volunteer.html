Volunteer with Saintly!

WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR?

A Community of Student Leaders

Saintly is constantly growing and trying to reach new people. Our team would heavily appreciate support in development and marketing. Saintly is still in its early stages and thus is not looking to fill specified roles and rather seeks those with initiative and leadership who could function as valuable additions to the Saintly team. We are especially looking for marketing, SEO, and backend specialists, although we are willing to adapt our work to fit your skills. If you want to apply, please fill out the form below.

WHY VOLUNTEER WITH SAINTLY?

Frontrow Seats to a New Era of Education

Volunteering with us is a great way to showinvolvement and depth by helping your community in a new and advanced way. By just using Saintly, you are furthering our mission of making learning free for all, an ideal we should all strive to cultivate. Saintly also gives you an opportunity to showcase and grow your mathematic aptitude and join a group of impactful, innovative, and driven student leaders.

HOW CAN YOU HELP SAINTLY GROW?

Become a Partner in a Global Competititon Math Network

There are three main ways to support Saintly. The first is to be chosen for Saintly's board and become a partner. These roles collaborate to plan adjustments and advances in Saintly, as well as have specialized roles to promote the platform. If you are not chosen, or simply do not have interest in having a higher role, you can also contribute by submitting solutions to practice problems, allowing our users to see more depth and other ways of solving the same problem. If you do not wish to contribute solutions, you can also be a passive user of saintly, which is help enough on its own, or contribute even further by spreading the word about Saintly on your social platforms or telling friends and family about our program
