WHY SAINTLY?

A New Approach to Competition Math

There have been significant strides to make typical, baseline education free. While that is a thorough effort, a lot elite competitions have been left behind. Saintly is built to bridge that gap by introducing first-of-its-kind interactive and free competition math help for all students.

WHO ARE WE?

Real, Dedicated Highschoolers

Meet the creators of Saintly Math!

Adalynn Le

Hi! I'm Adalynn, the founder of Saintly. I'm a freshman at Santa Teresa Highschool. When I was studying for my first AMC 10, I felt behind my friends who took expensive paid classes, classes I didn't have access to. I created Saintly as a free and helpful resource so that nobody would have to feel that way again. I hope you enjoy using Saintly!

WHY DID WE BUILD SAINTLY?

A Project Turned Product

Shortly after I finished my first AMC 10 Math Competition, I realized that self-studying hadn't been enough. I didn't know how, where, and what to study. That being said, I wasn't about to ask my parents to spend thousands on expensive tutors. From there, Saintly was born. As a project to help my high school math club, I named Saintly after my school (Santa Teresa) and built it around providing Santa Teresa students with free, quality education. About halfway through developing the project, I realized Saintly had much bigger applications. I've since been working on scaling Saintly into a global tool that others from around the world can use. Saintly currently has 230+ users across over 10 countries
