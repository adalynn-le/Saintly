WHAT IS SAINTLY?

100% Rigorous Prep, 0% Cost

Saintly is a free program built to make Olympiads, competitions, and learning accessible to all students. Saintly delivers free, high-quality content in the form of real AMC 10/12 practice problems and comprehensive solutions written by students, for students. We're driven by a mission to make competition math so friendly, engaging, and accessible that it feels saintly

WHY SAINTLY?

A New Approach to Competition Math

There have been significant strides to make typical, baseline education free. While that is a thorough effort, a lot elite competitions have been left behind. Saintly is built to bridge that gap by introducing first-of-its-kind interactive and free competition math help for all students.

WHY DID WE BUILD SAINTLY?

A Project Turned Product

Shortly after I finished my first AMC 10 Math Competition, I realized that self-studying hadn't been enough. I didn't know how, where, and what to study. That being said, I wasn't about to ask my parents to spend thousands on expensive tutors. From there, Saintly was born. As a project to help my high school math club, I named Saintly after my school (Santa Teresa) and built it around providing Santa Teresa students with free, quality education. About halfway through developing the project, I realized Saintly had much bigger applications. I've since been working on scaling Saintly into a global tool that others from around the world can use.
